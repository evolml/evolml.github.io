About Competition

Are you passionate about artificial intelligence, machine learning, or bio-inspired algorithms? Do you have ground-breaking ideas waiting to be shared with the world? This is your chance to showcase your creativity, innovation, and technical insight in a visually compelling format! Join our Poster Competition centered on the cutting-edge theme: “Present Innovative Ideas about Machine Learning and Evolutionary Algorithms” From novel applications to theoretical advancements, from hybrid models to ethical considerations we invite students, researchers, and AI enthusiasts to explore, express, and exhibit their ideas through well-designed posters.

Where? Department of Mathematics and Computing, Dr B R Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar, Punjab, India.

Suggested Topics

  • Cutting-edge ML models
  • ML + Evolutionary Algorithm hybrids
  • Explainable AI systems
  • Genetic Algorithms, PSO, DE optimization
  • Applications in healthcare, finance, robotics
  • Fairness and robustness in ML
  • Ethical challenges and future trends

Important Dates

  • Registration & Submission: 15 June 2025
  • Event & Judging: 19 June 2025
  • Results: 20 June 2025

Prizes & Recognition

  • Top 3 posters win certificates + prizes
  • Showcase on website & social media
  • Pitch to AI/ML experts

Guidelines

  • Align with competition theme
  • A1 (594 x 841 mm), vertical preferred
  • PDF or high-res PNG/JPG
  • Include Title, Author(s), Affiliation, Abstract
