About the Competition

Are you passionate about artificial intelligence, machine learning, or bio-inspired algorithms? Do you have ground-breaking ideas waiting to be shared with the world? This is your chance to showcase your creativity, innovation, and technical insight in a visually compelling format! Join our Poster Competition centered on the cutting-edge theme: “Present Innovative Ideas about Machine Learning and Evolutionary Algorithms” From novel applications to theoretical advancements, from hybrid models to ethical considerations we invite students, researchers, and AI enthusiasts to explore, express, and exhibitstrong> their ideas through well-designed posters
